Who We Are

Who We Are

Halifax Roofing Systems is a Limited Liability Company based in Accra, Ghana. It was Incorporated in Ghana in APRIL, 2010. During this time the Company has earned an excellent reputation and frequently carries out large projects for Private clients, Local Authorities, Housing Associations, Construction Companies, Churches and Government Agencies. Halifax Roofing Systems provides Clean ColorBond ® Roofing Materials and TrueCore ® Steel Trusses of a Higher International Standard.

Mission Statement

At Halifax Roofing Systems, we are committed to becoming a global business interface between Ghana and the rest of Africa in the area of the manufacturing of modern quality standard roofing sheets, refer to as the Standing Seam System as well as fabrication and assembling of Metal Trusses and the delivery of high-level quality installation services to our cherished clients

Vision

To be the number one manufacturer of high-level quality roofing materials and metal trusses that will serve the Ghanaian market and the rest of the sub region.

Our Products

We use high quality standard raw materials that are outsourced from BlueScope Steel, one of the world’s renowned steel companies in Australia. Our products are made to meet International Specifications and have been manufactured with anti-corrosive properties that prevent the Material from rusting. Halifax Roofing Systems produces aluminum and aluzinc long span, shot span, IDT(Internal Deep Through) I.B.R roofing sheets and Trusses. Our sheets come in various Profiles, Gauges (thickness), colours and accessories designed with elegance and class to give customers’ buildings a unique aesthetic.

Our Core Values

At Halifax Investment Ltd, we treat our customers with honesty, integrity and the highest level of service in the industry sector. We strive to be innovative through continuous learning in order to deliver products and services that exceed our customer expectations.
